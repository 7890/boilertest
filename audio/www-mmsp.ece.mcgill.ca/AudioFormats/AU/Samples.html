AU Sample Files

Sample Files from CopyAudio

The CopyAudio program (part of the AFsp package) can produce output files of a number of different types. Samples files produced by converting stereo 16-bit data are as follows.

M1F1-Alaw-AFsp.au (46 kB)
AU file, stereo A-law data
M1F1-mulaw-AFsp.au (46 kB)
AU file, stereo µ-law data
M1F1-int8-AFsp.au (46 kB)
AU file, stereo 8-bit data
M1F1-int16-AFsp.au (92 kB)
AU file, stereo 16-bit data
M1F1-int24-AFsp.au (138 kB)
AU file, stereo 24-bit data
M1F1-int32-AFsp.au (184 kB)
AU file, stereo 32-bit data
M1F1-float32-AFsp.au (184 kB)
AU file, stereo 32-bit float data
M1F1-float64-AFsp.au (368 kB)
AU file, stereo 64-bit float data

Perverse Files

The following WAVE files are perverse or have problems.

monkey.au (2 kB)
AU file, µ-law data, with header length 24, should be 28 minimum.
soundB.au (85 kB)
AU file, µ-law data, with bad data length (twice actual value)
